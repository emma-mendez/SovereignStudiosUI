  • FAQ
  • What’s included in the £275 fee?
    Private use of the entire venue (10hrs, 2pm–midnight) 9 XL tables & 80 chairs Professional sound system & lighting BYO food, drinks, decorations
  • Can I view the space before booking?
    Yes! Self-viewings available with 1hr notice. Request access via enquiry form.
  • Do you allow external caterers/DJs?
    Absolutely – bring your own (no kitchen on-site).
  • What’s your capacity?
    100 standing / 55 seated
  • Parking available?
    No on-site parking, but free street parking 2-min walk away (no restrictions after 6pm/weekends).
  • Can I book outside 2pm–midnight?
    - Early access only: £10.50/hr (before 2pm).
    - No charge for events ending past midnight (must finish by 6am)
  • Security for parties?
    Security is required for 18-25th birthdays where alcohol is available (2 guard minimum, arranged by client/in-house reccomended company) Must be SIA Badged.
  • How many security guards required?
    Under-25 events: 1-20 guests: 2 security guards required 21-50 guests: 3 security guards 51+ guests: 4 security guards. A quote will be provided with an enquiry.
  • Is alcohol allowed?
    Allowed for private parties only (no alcohol sales permitted).
